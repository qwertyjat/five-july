About Us

Baby Doll Makeover

Baby Doll Makeover is Sikar's premium beauty destination, built around one simple belief — every woman deserves to feel like her most radiant self. From bridal transformations to everyday grooming, our studio blends modern technique with an unhurried, personal touch.

Our expert stylists and makeup artists train continually to stay ahead of trends, while every tool and space is maintained to the highest standard of hygiene and care. We take pride in listening first, then creating a look that feels authentically you.

Step in for a quick touch-up or a full bridal experience — you'll leave feeling pampered, polished, and unmistakably beautiful.
